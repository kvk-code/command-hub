## 🎯 PEDAGOGICAL STRATEGY

### **Learning Design Principles Applied**

**1. Scaffolding**
- Part 1: Foundational concepts (delivered)
- Interactive reinforcement: Games (provided now)
- Part 2: Advanced concepts (upcoming with Dr. Mohan)

**2. Active Learning**
- Students engage with concepts through hands-on interaction
- Games provide immediate feedback
- Self-paced exploration encourages autonomy

**3. Cognitive Mapping**
- Explicit connections between games and slides
- Helps students build mental models
- Reinforces key concepts from presentation

**4. Gamification**
- Makes abstract concepts (hashing, immutability) tangible
- Increases engagement through interactive elements
- Reduces cognitive load through playful learning

**5. Spiral Learning**
- Concepts introduced in presentation
- Reinforced through games
- Will be deepened in Part 2 session

**Hash Chain Simulator → Blockchain Immutability**
- **Concept:** Once data is added to blockchain, changing it affects all subsequent blocks
- **Presentation Coverage:** Slide 13 (blockchain structure)

- **Presentation Coverage:** Slide 9 (system architectures)
- **Game Mechanism:** Classification exercise with feedback
- **Learning Outcome:** Ability to distinguish between system types
